# Member Guide

This page explains the member-facing flow inside the verification hub.

## Start a verification

1. Open the verification hub post in the subreddit.
2. Review your current status near the top of the page.
3. Click `Photo Instructions` and read the subreddit-specific instructions.
4. Click `Submit Verification`.
5. Confirm all submission statements:
   - you are at least 18
   - the uploaded photos are of you and do not depict anyone under 18
   - you accept the VouchX Terms and Conditions
6. Upload the required number of photos and submit the form.

The number of required photos is set by the subreddit moderators and can be 1, 2, or 3.

## Statuses

| Label | What it means | What you can do next |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `Not verified` | You do not currently have an active verification. | Review `Photo Instructions` and submit if verifications are enabled. |
| `Pending review` | A moderator has not reviewed your request yet. | Wait for review or use `Withdraw Pending Verification` to cancel it. |
| `Pending re-review` | A moderator reopened a denied case and moved it back into the queue. | Wait for review or use `Withdraw Pending Verification` to cancel it. |
| `Verified` | Your account is verified through the configured VouchX flair check. | No action is required. You can use `Remove Verification` if you want to remove it. |
| `Verified (Manual)` | Your account is being treated as verified through the flair CSS fallback instead of a direct template-ID match. | No action is required. You can use `Remove Verification` if you want to remove it. |
| `Blocked` | You cannot submit new verification requests in this subreddit. This affects verification submissions only, not posting in the subreddit. | Only a moderator can remove the block. |

## While a request is pending

- The hub shows the latest submission details.
- `Withdraw Pending Verification` removes the pending request.
- The hub refreshes automatically while open, so status changes appear without reopening the post.

## If the request is denied

- The latest submission shows the denial reason label chosen by the moderators.
- Members can submit again unless they have been blocked due to 3 denials or manual moderator block.
- In some cases moderators can reopen the denied case for re-review instead of requiring a new upload.

## If the request is approved

- The configured verification flair is applied.
- The app shows the account as verified based on flair detection.
- Members can later click `Remove Verification` to remove their verified status and stored verification data.

## When submissions are unavailable

Members cannot submit when:

- the subreddit has disabled verifications
- the account is blocked from submitting
- the account already has a pending request

If submissions are disabled, the hub shows the subreddit’s configured disabled message.
